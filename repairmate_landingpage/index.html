About Us

Our Story

RepairMate emerged from the shared industrial experience of our team—Xavier Cruz, John López, and Abimael Pérez. Working in various industrial environments, they encountered the persistent challenges of managing machine repairs, documenting procedures, and onboarding new team members. This firsthand exposure highlighted a critical need for a streamlined, effective approach to machine maintenance and staff training.

What began as a solution to these everyday challenges has blossomed into RepairMate—a comprehensive platform transforming how businesses approach maintenance and skill-building. Developed as our Portfolio Project at Holberton School, RepairMate embodies our dedication to leveraging industry insights into a practical, powerful tool that supports businesses worldwide.
